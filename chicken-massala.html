Chicken Massala



Recipe for chicken massala

Before we start making the chicken massala we have to order our ingredients first. The following ingredients can be found nearby grocery stores or indian shops if there is one nearby.

Ingredients

  • massala powder
  • paprika
  • 500g chicken
  • rice 500g
  • salt
  • scoop of tikka powder

After we got our ingredients we can start by making the ingredients ready to cook our hot and spicy chicken massala. I'll explain the steps you'll have to follow right below.

  1. First make sure every ingredients is shown and separated with the others.
  2. After separating make sure that the chicken is in front of you. Start cutting the chicken in small pieces.
  3. Take the massala and tikka powder and squeeze it in the chicken by mixing it with your hands.
  4. Take the paprika and cut it in pieces with a knife. After cutting the paprika put it in witht he powdered chicken in a bowl.
  5. Take the salt and put 3 clockwise rounds of it in the mixed bowl with chicken.
